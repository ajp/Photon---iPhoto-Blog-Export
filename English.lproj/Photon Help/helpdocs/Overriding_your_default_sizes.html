Overriding your default sizes

There may be some occasions that your default ‘max width’ and ‘max height’ settings are inappropriate, and you wish to provide different values. Photon’s main window features ‘max width’ and ‘max height’ fields of its own that allow you to override those defaults on a case–by–case basis.

To override your default size settings, enter any value into the ‘max width’ or ‘max height’ field of Photon’s main window.

The overriding value not carried over to your next export.
