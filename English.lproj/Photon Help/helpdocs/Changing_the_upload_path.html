Changing the upload path

By default, Photon uploads images to the main directory of your weblog as defined by your blogging software’s settings. Photon’s ‘Upload path’ field lets you specify a custom upload path; uploading your images to any subdirectory of that default blog directory.

For example, if your blog directory is http://www.example.com/blog/, specifying an upload path of my/great/photos/ would upload your photos to http://www.example.com/blog/my/great/photos/

To upload your exported photos to a custom subdirectory of your blog’s main directory, specify the path in the ‘Upload path’ field of the Image Upload tab in Photon’s Settings dialog.

If you specify a directory that does not already exist on the server, your blogging software will create it.
