Assigning photos to multiple categories

When posting photos to your weblog, you may wish to assign them to several categories of that blog at once.

To assign the exported photos to any number of categories in the selected weblog, choose ‘Select Multiple…’ from the pop–up menu labeled ‘Categories’ in Photon’s main window.

A dialog with a scrolling list of your categories will prompt you to make your selection. Check the categories you wish to export to and press ‘Use Selected Categories’.
