Category retrieval failed

If Photon cannot fetch your category listing from the server, it is most likely due to a loss of internet connection. In these cases a small “lightning bolt” button will appear to the right of the pop–up menu labeled ‘Category’ in Photon’s main window to indicate that you are now working offline.

If you are working offline and want to attempt server connection again, click the “lightning bolt” button.

Clicking the “lightning bolt” button will attempt another connection with the server. If a successful connection is made, Photon will retrieve your category listing.

If Photon still cannot retrieve your categories from the server, check your internet connection and try again.
