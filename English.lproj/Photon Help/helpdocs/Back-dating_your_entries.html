Back–dating your entries

By default, blog entries are stamped with the date and time they are created by the blogging software. To maintain a true chronology in your photo–blog, you may prefer to have Photon adjust these time stamps to reflect the time–codes stored in iPhoto.

This way your blog entries will be stamped with the date and time the photo itself was taken, rather than the date and time they are exported from iPhoto.

To date your blog entries with the time–code information kept by iPhoto, check the box labeled “Use iPhoto time stamp for entry date & time” in the Entry Creation tab of Photon’s Settings dialog.
