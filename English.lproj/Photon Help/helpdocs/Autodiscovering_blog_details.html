Autodiscovering blog details

Out of the box, Movable Type™ and TypePad™ blogs build an RSD (Really Simple Discovery) file that discloses some of the otherwise–obscure details of your blog that are required by Photon.

If your blog has an RSD file, you needn’t fill in the ‘Blog ID’, ‘Access Point’, or ‘Platform’ fields in Photon’s Account Information tab; this information can be discovered automatically.

During account creation, press the ‘Autodiscover’ button to the right of the ‘Blog ID’ field in the Account Information tab.

If RSD retrieval is successful, the ‘Blog ID’, ‘Access Point’ and ‘Platform’ fields will be filled in automatically.

If retrieval fails, or if you choose not to initiate autodiscovery, you will need to fill in these fields manually.
