Categories

Weblog entries are typically assigned to a category that describes the theme or topic discussed in the entry. In photo–blogs, however, categories reflect the event or subject portrayed in the photo; similar to the way we use Photo Albums in iPhoto. Use Photon to create and manage categories for your photo–blog.
